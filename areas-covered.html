Kitchen Fitting in Portsmouth

Portsmouth is our central operating hub. From the historic residential streets of PO1 to the newer developments overlooking Gunwharf Quays, we install modern, space-optimizing open-plan kitchens tailored for active city living. Our team is familiar with the structural details of local Portsmouth buildings, allowing us to complete installations quickly and coordinate plumbing and electrical hookups without delay.

Kitchen Installation in Southsea

Southsea is famous for its stunning Victorian and Edwardian terraced townhouses. Fitting kitchens in these older properties requires specialist carpentry experience. We have fitted hundreds of shaker-style kitchens here, custom-routing panels to clear out-of-square walls, adjusting structural trim around original chimney breasts, and matching cabinets to the high ceilings and bay-window layouts characteristic of Southsea architecture.

Kitchen Fitters in Fareham

Fareham features a diverse mix of spacious detached family homes and modern suburban estates. We regularly work in Fareham, Portchester, and Wallington, providing full-service "Supply & Fit" options. Homeowners here often choose our high-end shaker designs or contemporary gloss handleless setups, paired with solid oak or quartz worktops to maximize property value.

Kitchen Refurbishment in Gosport

Located across Portsmouth Harbour, Gosport is home to busy families and maritime retirees in neighborhoods like Alverstoke, Elson, and Hardway. We provide cost-effective kitchen facelifts and full installations here. We specialize in designing space-saving galley kitchen layouts and integrating clever under-counter storage solutions to make the most of Gosport's coastal homes.

New Kitchens in Waterlooville

Heading north of the Portsmouth ports, Waterlooville is defined by large new-build estates and spacious 1970s suburban housing. We work extensively in Waterlooville, installing contemporary handleless kitchens and carrying out large-scale kitchen extensions. Homeowners here benefit from our trade discounts on premium composite surfaces, custom LED profiles, and integrated smart appliances.
