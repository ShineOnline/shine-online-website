Our Story

About Us — Passionate About Perfect Kitchens

Portsmouth Kitchen Fitting Co. was founded by Steve Miller, a local carpenter with a singular goal: to provide Hampshire homeowners with a premium, reliable kitchen fitting service built on honest craftsmanship. Steve started his career over 15 years ago as an apprentice carpenter in the Portsmouth shipyards, learning the value of structural precision and meticulous attention to detail before moving into residential construction and kitchen specialization.

Over the years, Steve noticed that many high street kitchen suppliers sub-contracted their fitting projects to rushed installers. These installers were paid by the carcass, encouraging speed over quality. In response, Steve established Portsmouth Kitchen Fitting Co., creating a small, dedicated team of local tradesmen who share his commitment to doing things right the first time.

We pride ourselves on our strict policy of never using random sub-contracted installers. Every member of our fitting crew is directly employed by the business, fully vetted, and works under Steve's direct on-site supervision. This means that we take full ownership of the quality, safety, and cleanliness of your renovation from the day we rip out your old cabinets to the moment we hand you the keys to your new kitchen.

We believe in treating your home like our own. Our fitters lay down heavy-duty protective sheets on your flooring, use active dust extraction systems on our carpentry saws, and sweep up at the end of every day. We are proud of our local Portsmouth heritage and work hard to maintain the 5-star reputation we have earned from Southsea to Waterlooville.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide our work, our customer relationships, and our team on a daily basis.

Honest Pricing

We believe in absolute transparency. The price we quote is the price you pay. There are no hidden fees, surprise material costs, or unexpected adjustments at the end of the project. If structural surprises arise, we discuss solutions with you first.

Clean & Respectful

A kitchen renovation is naturally messy, but we work hard to keep it controlled. We treat your property with the utmost care, minimizing dust flow into surrounding rooms, protecting your hallways, and leaving your home safe and tidy every evening.

Built to Last

We do not take shortcuts. From sealing worktop undersides around dishwashers to double-securing structural corner frames, we build kitchens designed to withstand the wear and tear of active family life for decades to come.
