Common Questions Before Inquiring

A few quick answers to help you prepare before contacting Steve's fitting team.

Do I need a pre-existing design?

No, not at all! If you have Wren or Howdens drawings, we'll work from them. If you only have a blank room and an idea, we can help design your kitchen layout from scratch using professional trade measurement guides.

What do your consultations cost?

Every single site inspection, consultation, 3D verification check, and detailed written quotation is 100% free of charge. We believe in providing transparent pricing upfront to let you make the right choice.

What is your project lead time?

Our schedule changes seasonally, but we typically book kitchen fittings 4 to 8 weeks in advance. We recommend reaching out as early as possible so we can align our carpenter calendars with your delivery date.
